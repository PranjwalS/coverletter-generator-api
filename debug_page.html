About the job

Overview Are you someone who is passionate, motivated, and driven to make a difference? If so, MSA Safety is the perfect fit for your career.

At MSA, SAFETY is who we are AND it is what we do. We are a purpose-driven company committed to deploying innovation and technology to deliver on our Mission to help protect people and assets all around the world. We continue to be relentless in our pursuit of solving our customers greatest problems so they can go home safe each and every day.

Are you in? Read on for more details about this particular role.

Responsibilities

  • Completing New Product Development (NPD) testing and creating corresponding test reports
  • Developing solutions to problems based on Root Cause Analysis (RCA)
  • 3D Prototyping novel concepts and designs
  • Developing test specifications and designing test fixtures
  • Collaborating with international associates as needed to complete project goals
Qualifications
  • Hands-on technical aptitude for product design, development, and evaluation
  • Strong analytical, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills
  • Excellent written, verbal, and presentation skills
  • Ability to work in a team environment
  • Strong organizational and multi-tasking skills and a sharp attention to detail
  • Appreciation for Safety
Must be available for the Fall 2026 semester, full-time, from August-December.

MSA Safety Incorporated (NYSE: MSA) is the global leader in advanced safety products, technologies and solutions. Driven by its singular mission of safety, the Company has been at the forefront of safety innovation since 1914, protecting workers and facility infrastructure around the world across a broad range of diverse end markets while creating sustainable value for shareholders. With 2023 revenues of $1.8 billion, MSA Safety is headquartered in Cranberry Township, Pennsylvania and employs a team of over 5,000 associates across its more than 40 international locations.
